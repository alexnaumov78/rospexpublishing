Dmitry V. Naumov is a physicist who works both as a theorist and experimentalist, a rare combination in modern physics. He is a leading expert in neutrino physics, elementary particle physics, and astrophysics, and heads the Neutrino Programme at JINR. He has lectured on quantum field theory at Moscow State University and JINR for over 20 years.
